Who we are

We are Niwendo Studios, or simply Niwendo. We create our own games, experiment with technology, and build things that we enjoy making. Sometimes we might take things a little too far and end up creating our own engine for a small game just because we felt like it. Why? Because we can, and because we love exploring new ideas. Niwendo is built by a small team of developers and creatives who are passionate about bringing their own ideas to life.

What we do

Our main focus is game development. We don’t just want to create games. We also like experimenting with new systems, developing our own technologies, and turning creative ideas into real projects.
